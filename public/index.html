How It Works

We aim to empower game-developers to embrace player-driven
innovation through our dynamic voting system.

We aim to empower game developers by implementing a dynamic voting system that incentivizes gamers to act as a Decentralized Autonomous Organization.
The more players indulge or progress in the game, the more rewarding it is for them as the weightaging their vote increases.
The more weight their vote holds, the more likely their vote will be to influence the game's mechanics and features.
